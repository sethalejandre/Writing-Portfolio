Draft Process Artifact 2

Outline:
I. Introduction
- Hook and background information
- Thesis statement on the importance of comprehensive mental health programs on college campuses
II. Mental Health Challenges Among College Students
- Discuss the pressure to excel academically and financial stress
- Highlight the impact on mental well-being
III. The Link Between Mental Health and Academic Performance
- Explore how mental health affects concentration, learning, and academic achievement
- Present research on the correlation between mental health and GPA/dropout rates
IV. The Need for Comprehensive Mental Health Programs
- Discuss the current lack of support and resources
- Present evidence on the benefits of comprehensive programs
V. Creating a Supportive Environment
- Discuss strategies for fostering a supportive campus culture
- Highlight the importance of accessible counseling services
VI. Conclusion
- Reinforce the urgency of implementing comprehensive mental health programs on California college campuses
- Call to action for stakeholders

Reflection

The outline played a vital role in organizing my thoughts and structuring my persuasive essay. It provided a clear roadmap for developing each section and ensuring a logical flow of ideas. The introduction effectively grabbed the reader's attention, and the thesis statement clearly conveyed my stance on the importance of comprehensive mental health programs. Each section of the outline allowed me to delve deeper into the mental health challenges faced by college students, the impact on academic performance, the need for comprehensive programs, and strategies for creating a supportive environment. The outline ensured a logical flow of ideas and helped me stay focused on my main arguments throughout the drafting process.
