About Myself

My name is Seth Alejandre, and I'm currently a 3rd-year software engineering student at Cal Poly State University. While I may not consider myself an avid reader or writer, I do recognize the importance of these skills in my academic journey. As a STEM enthusiast, my primary interests lie in the world of technology and problem-solving. However, I've come to appreciate the value of effective communication through writing. It may not be my natural inclination, but I've discovered that honing my writing skills opens up new opportunities for expression and connection. Originally from the vibrant city of Las Vegas, I have a passion for exploring new places and immersing myself in diverse experiences. Whether it's trying out local cuisine or embarking on thrilling adventures, I enjoy creating lasting memories that shape my outlook on life.
